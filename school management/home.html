Vision

At ABC School, it is our objective to establish a study culture that fosters diligence, sincerity and accountability which helps our students to develop a passion for learning that will serve them throughout and beyond their school years. We endeavour to promote the pursuit of excellence and achievement for every student to enable them to perform to their potential.



Academics

we believe that children must receive a thorough grounding of whatever concepts they learn and that this learning has to be integrated with life. The motto of the school is, ‘maximum growth of each learner’.Therefore, we have a self-designed curriculum, which has evolved after years of research and expertise. The curriculum is based on a Thematic or Integrated approach to studying.



Infrastructure

Targeting a superior educational environment, spacious, well- ventilated and networked class rooms, labs, audio-visual studios, music and dance room along with other amenities needed to provide a good learning experience are made available. The learning atmosphere is livened with brightly painted furniture and colourful display boards.
